FOUNDER & ACADEMIC MENTOR – EMMBEE.ONE

I am an Information Technology professional with nearly two decades of global industry experience across India, Japan, and France.

Over the years, I have worked in roles such as Solution Architect, Mentor, Trainer, Cybersecurity Consultant, Design Thinker and Artificial Intelligence researcher — helping organizations solve complex problems and build reliable systems.

A Global Realization

"Success in life depends not only on academic marks, but on clarity of thinking, confidence in communication, and the ability to solve problems independently."

Through my international work, I have often mentored young professionals and students, helping them develop structured thinking and logical reasoning. These experiences inspired me to contribute at an earlier stage — when students are forming their academic habits.

My approach is heavily influenced by Design Thinking principles — encouraging students to ask questions, think independently, and develop problem-solving skills rather than relying only on memorization.

Having returned to India, I intend to share this practical knowledge and global perspective. I intentionally keep batch sizes small (max 10) so that every student receives the individual attention and steady guidance they deserve.
