**Dear Class 10 Student & Parent,**

Exam stress is completely normal — and the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has officially recognised it. This kit is built 100% from CBSE's own stress-management resources so you can face the 2026 boards with clarity and confidence.

</div>

<div class="section">

## <span class="icon">📌</span> CBSE's Official 5 A's to Control Exam Stress

<div class="list-item">

<span class="num">1</span>**Acknowledge** – Accept that stress is natural. Every student feels it.

</div>

<div class="list-item">

<span class="num">2</span>**Appreciate** – Focus on what you have already prepared instead of what is left.

</div>

<div class="list-item">

<span class="num">3</span>**Alleviate** – Use relaxation techniques (deep breathing, short breaks).

</div>

<div class="list-item">

<span class="num">4</span>**Alter** – Change negative thoughts into realistic ones.

</div>

<div class="list-item">

<span class="num">5</span>**Avoid** – Avoid last-minute cramming and comparison with others.

</div>

</div>

<div class="section">

## <span class="icon">🧘</span> 5 Instant Relief Techniques (CBSE Recommended)

<div class="card blue tech-box">

<span class="tech-title">1. Deep Breathing (5 seconds)</span> Shut your eyes → Inhale for 4 counts → Hold 4 → Exhale 6. Repeat 5 times. CBSE calls this "the quickest and most effective way".

</div>

<div class="card blue tech-box">

<span class="tech-title">2. Progressive Muscle Relaxation</span> Tighten and release each muscle group (toes → face) for 5 seconds each. Reduces physical tension instantly.

</div>

<div class="card blue tech-box">

<span class="tech-title">3. 4-7-8 Breathing</span> Keep inhaling for 4 seconds → Hold breath for 7 seconds → Exhale slowly in 8 seconds. CBSE-endorsed relaxation technique.

</div>

<div class="card blue">

<span class="tech-title">4. Positive Self-Talk</span> Replace "I will fail" with "I am prepared and I will do my best".

</div>

</div>

<div class="section">

## <span class="icon">📅</span> 7-Day Pre-Exam Routine (CBSE Approved)

<div class="routine-grid">

<div class="routine-item">

🌅 Sleep 7–8 hours every night

</div>

<div class="routine-item">

🥗 Eat balanced meals + stay hydrated

</div>

<div class="routine-item">

🚶 20–30 minutes light exercise or walk daily

</div>

<div class="routine-item">

📝 Make a realistic timetable – 6–8 hours study with breaks

</div>

<div class="routine-item">

🧘 10 minutes yoga or meditation

</div>

</div>
